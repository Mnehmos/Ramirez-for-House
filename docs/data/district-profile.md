## Key Economic Data

### Graham County
- Population: 40,107
- GDP: $1.7 billion (ranks 15th of 15 in per capita income)
- Cotton farming: 54x national average employment share
- Poverty rate: 17.7%
- Largest employer: Freeport-McMoRan

### Greenlee County
- GDP: 90% from mining
- Poverty rate: 17.7% (47% above national average)
- Voted Democratic 1912-1996 (until union was broken)

### Vail
- Median household income: $121,385
- Rejected incorporation in 2023 — distrust of taxes
- Conservative-leaning but potential swing territory

### Green Valley
- Median age: 73.3
- 68% rely on retirement income
- 100+ HOAs, volunteer governance
- Water table declining due to mining/agricultural pumping
