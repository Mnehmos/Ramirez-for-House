## Arizona Operations

### Morenci Mine (Greenlee County)
- Largest copper mine in North America
- Unions decertified in 1984 after 1983 strike
- 2012: Freeport paid $6.8M to settle federal charges for acidic metal-bearing water outflows injuring waters, soils, habitats, and birds

### Safford/Lone Star Mine (Graham County)
- Targeting 300 million pounds of copper per year from oxide ores
- Largest private-sector employer in the SEAGO region
